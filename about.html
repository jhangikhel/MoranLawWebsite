Moram & James Law Associates

Moram and James Law Associates, LLC provide legal representatives in the U.S. immigration laws and regulations That are made by Congress at the state level and are under the control of the state. We provide legal advice and representations to businesses and individuals wishing to enter the United States through temporary visas that allow employment (H-1B, TN, L-1, etc.), work-based green cards (PERM employee certificate, 140, Fixing Status), Family-based options (130, K visa, etc.), Naturalization applications and other immigration options. We represent a wide range of clients from multinational corporations to start-up organizations, private organizations in government agencies, health care providers in educational institutions, skilled and qualified people, and students, visitors, religious workers. We respond to the diverse needs of our customers by offering a variety of unique and customized services based on our experience, expertise, and knowledge. The legal services we provide are of the highest standard as we strive to provide prompt, decent and cost-effective assistance in line with the highest ethical standards. We maintain regular contact with our customers regarding their specific circumstances and answer any questions as soon as possible. We look forward to discuss your situation with you and working together to find the right solution for your immigration needs.
